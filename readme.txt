== Frequently Asked Questions ==

#### What happened to my website after I installed Rhythms?

It's been optimized and you are now reading so fast your brain can't keep up. Keep reading. Your brain needs to get used to ingesting information at this rapid pace. If you start to feel light headed we'd recommend reading a regular book for a while while sipping cold water.
